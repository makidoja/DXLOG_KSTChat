
Download DXLogKstBridge.dll and place in your %appdata%\DXLog.net\CustomForms folder

Open DXLog and setup a contest

On the top bar you should see Custom > KST Chat Bridge, click on this

Click setup, add your KSTChat username and password, along with your name and locator and room to connect to then OK

Click on Connect, this will connect to the room set and populate the left hand wisde with all the online stations in the room

The right top window will show messages

Click on a station, then in the bottom half of the right pane this will show only messages to that station

Adjust your colours, position and title bar colour with Right Click in the window (same as other DXLog windows)

With a station highlighted, you can click "To call" button and that will open a free text box to send a message to that station

With a station highlighted, you can click a Macro button and it will send the macro message to that station, you can include Frequency and Mode from the radio connected to the log with {FREQ} {MODE}

Click the Edit Macros button to edit the templates

Click the Map button, this will open a map in a seperate window, so that can be moved onto a second monitor, clicking on a statioon will add the callsign to the log line and the QRA locator into DXLog, 
If Turn Rotator On is ticked and a rotator is in DXLOG for that band, it will also turn the antenna to the QRA.

The map can be zoomed or grab and move with the mouse

You can change rooms with the room button
